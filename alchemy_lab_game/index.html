SYNTHESIS LADDER

ALCHEMY LAB

Bottle the escalating tension of a merge ladder layered with decay and reactor pressure. Every placement is a bet against the heat and the clock.

Goal: Synthesize Uranium (U) — the top of a 16-rung ladder.
Move: Drag-and-drop an isotope onto an identical one to fuse into the next tier.
Reactor heat: Rises while you idle. When full it warns, then auto-merges a random pair.
Decay: Higher tiers (Zn, Ag, Au) vanish after ~12s if neglected — watch the countdown.
Score: The highest element tier you reach. It persists across sessions.
Lose: Board full with no possible merges.
