Principles

What Makes This Loop Work

The orchestration loop isn't just a sequence — it's a set of principles that ensure every discussion produces real value.

🚫

No Skipping Phases

The Moderator enforces phase boundaries. You can't Execute without Narrowing, and you can't Narrow without Exploring. Each phase builds on the last.

🔄

Controlled Backtracking

If new information surfaces during Narrow, the group can return to Explore — but only with the Moderator's explicit call. Backtracking is deliberate, not accidental.

✅

Evidence Over Opinion

The Librarian grounds claims in prior art. The Cynical agent demands proof. Opinions are welcome, but they must be supported — especially during Narrow and Execute.

📦

Always Ship Something

The Pragmatist ensures every discussion produces a tangible artifact — code, documentation, or a decision record. Discussions that end without deliverables are failures.

🛑

Explicit Sign-Off

No phase transition is implicit. The Moderator declares when the group moves forward, and only after checking that the current phase's objectives are met.

⚡

Progress Over Perfection

The loop prioritizes forward momentum. A good-enough decision that ships today beats a perfect decision that ships never. The Pragmatist enforces this bias for action.
